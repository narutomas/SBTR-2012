ABOUT

Every week one of us sets the theme and rules for a photo challenge. The theme sets the mood. The rules are more specific like : black & white, use a 50mm lens, an animal in frame, or fixed location, anything goes. We do this to challenge and inspire each other to move forward in our photography, and have some fun while we’re at it! We update every sunday. Updated every sunday.
